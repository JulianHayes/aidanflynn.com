## Key Findings

- **8 white spaces** identified where genuine demand exists and current supply is structurally inadequate
- **Primary positioning recommendation**: "The Regulated Experience House" — compliance-integrated experiential delivery for premium brands in regulated industries
- **Brand personality**: Precise. Cultured. Accountable.
- **Target verticals**: Financial services (28%), B2B tech (25%), luxury (22%)
- **UK disenchanted spend (base case)**: £525M (25% of £2.1BN UK market)
- **Year 1 revenue target**: £250K-£400K (3-5 clients at £50K-£100K)
- **Year 3 revenue target**: £1.2M-£2M
